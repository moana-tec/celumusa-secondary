Who We Are

Where every learner finds their voice.

Celumusa Secondary School stands as a beacon of education in KwaZulu-Natal, offering a holistic learning environment that honours each learner's potential. We believe that true education reaches beyond the classroom — it shapes character, builds resilience, and ignites a lifelong love of learning.

Our dedicated staff, modern facilities, and vibrant school culture work together to create an atmosphere where learners thrive academically, socially, and spiritually. We are proud of our heritage and ambitious about our future.
